Description
Algae World is an offline single-player cultivation puzzle where you control a light source illuminating a tank of algae colonies. Each algae species responds to light and nutrients differently, and every level challenges you to keep colonies healthy while completing compound orders before time runs out. HOW TO PLAY 1. Move the lamp Drag the light source around the tank. The HUD shows whether each algae is in GOOD, FED, or OVER-lit state. Direct, refracted, filtered, and dark zones each affect algae efficiency differently. 2. Feed nutrient capsules Tap N, P, or K capsules to dissolve nutrients into the water. Green algae uses nitrogen, cyanobacteria uses phosphorus, red algae uses potassium, and golden algae prefers balanced N/P/K. Nutrients decay exponentially over time, so timing matters. 3. Watch oxygen Algae produce oxygen during photosynthesis. In day/night levels (Phase 4 and beyond), oxygen drops at night. Keep the oxygen bar above zero to avoid colony damage. 4. Complete compound orders Each level has a target compound: chlorophyll, phycocyanin, phycoerythrin, carotenoid, omega-3, antioxidant, biodiesel, or the ultimate space nutrient. Grow the right algae to synthesize the compound and fill the order meter before the 30-second timer expires. 5. Earn stars Three stars: 80% survival rate and full target population. Two stars: 50% survival and half target. One star: any survivors. Zero stars if all algae die. 50 LEVELS ACROSS 5 PHASES Phase 1 (L1-L10): Green algae and chlorophyll orders. Learn light direction, optimal zones, and over-illumination. Phase 2 (L11-L20): Cyanobacteria joins with phycocyanin orders. Nutrient injection and species competition introduced. Phase 3 (L21-L30): Red algae and golden algae arrive. Phycoerythrin, carotenoid, omega-3, and antioxidant orders. Phase 4 (L31-L40): Day/night cycles begin. Oxygen pressure mechanic. Biodiesel and antioxidant synthesis. Phase 5 (L41-L50): Space cultivator challenge. All mechanisms combined. SpaceNutrient synthesis is the ultimate goal. FOUR ALGAE SPECIES - Green Algae: uses Nitrogen, thrives in direct light, produces Chlorophyll - Cyanobacteria: uses Phosphorus, prefers refracted light, produces Phycocyanin - Red Algae (Rhodophyta): uses Potassium, likes filtered light, produces Phycoerythrin - Golden Algae (Chrysophyta): balances N/P/K, moderate light, produces Carotenoid FULLY OFFLINE No internet connection required. No ads. No in-app purchases. No accounts. No analytics. No tracking. All progress saves locally on your device. KEY FEATURES - 50 hand-tuned levels across 5 progressive phases - 4 distinct algae species with unique nutrient and light preferences - 8 compound orders from chlorophyll to space nutrient - Light zone system: direct, refracted, filtered, dark - Exponential nutrient decay with half-life simulation - Day/night cycle with oxygen pressure (Phase 4+) - Star rating based on survival rate and population - Portrait-only, iPhone-only, fully offline - Calm, focused single-player gameplay
