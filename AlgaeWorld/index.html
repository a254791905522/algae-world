Algae World

Move the lamp, feed algae with nutrient capsules, and keep each colony in the right light zone.

Algae World is a single-player cultivation puzzle where light position, algae species, and N/P/K capsules work together. Each level asks you to protect colony health, grow the right algae, and complete compound orders before time runs out.

Gameplay

Control the lamp

Drag the light source around the tank. Direct, refracted, filtered, and dark zones change algae efficiency and health.

Use growth capsules

N, P, and K capsules dissolve into the water and support different algae types, making nutrients the base for colony growth.

Complete orders

Grow the right algae to synthesize chlorophyll, phycocyanin, carotenoids, omega-3, antioxidants, and later combined orders.
